The Gazelle | 2018

In 2018 Spring I was the Video Desk Editor of The Gazelle, a UAE newspaper.

I worked as an all around videographer on the productions, shooting and recording sound, as well as editing the videos. Recruited participants (10+) and conceptualized content that reaches the student body of 1200+.
